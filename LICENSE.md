## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

**❌ Script Does Not Execute After Inject**
The executor may not have attached to the correct Roblox process instance.
**Solution:** Close all Roblox windows, relaunch the game fresh, wait for full load, then re-attach your executor before executing the **Tha Bronx 3 Script** again.

---

**❌ GUI Overlay Not Visible In-Game**
The GUI may have spawned off-screen or failed to render due to a race condition.
**Solution:** Type `respawnGUI()` directly into your executor's script console and press Enter. The hub will reappear without requiring a full re-inject.

---

**❌ Money Dupe Stops After a Few Cycles**
Server-side rate limiting or a game update may have patched the loop timing.
**Solution:** Open your config and increase `MoneyDelay` to `0.5` or higher. Also ensure you're running the latest auto-updated build of the **Best Tha Bronx 3 Script**.

---

**❌ Android Executor Crashes After Inject**
Insufficient RAM or background apps are competing with the executor process.
**Solution:** Close all background apps before starting your **Tha Bronx 3 Script Android** session. Ensure the device has at least 3GB of free RAM available before injecting.

---

**❌ Gun Dupe Not Working on Certain Weapons**
Some weapon classes have server-side validation that blocks client dupe attempts.
**Solution:** Update to the latest script version — **Gun Dupe Tha Bronx 3 Script** patches are pushed frequently. Enable `DebugMode` in config to identify which weapon class is triggering the block.

---

**❌ Auto Rejoin Not Reconnecting**
Custom server teleport handlers can interfere with the default reconnect logic.
**Solution:** Disable Auto Rejoin in config, manually rejoin once, re-enable it, and re-execute the script. This resets the session reference the module uses for reconnection.
